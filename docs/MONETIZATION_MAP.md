## Key Metrics to Track

- **MRR** - Monthly Recurring Revenue
- **ARPU** - Average Revenue Per User
- **CAC** - Customer Acquisition Cost
- **LTV** - Lifetime Value
- **Churn Rate** - Monthly cancellations
- **NRR** - Net Revenue Retention
- **Affiliate CTR** - Click-through rate
- **Conversion Rate** - Visitor to subscriber

---

## Risk Mitigation

1. **Affiliate dependency**: Diversify partners, negotiate direct deals
2. **Churn spikes**: Implement retention campaigns, feedback loops
3. **Seasonality**: Build annual prepay incentives
4. **Competition**: Focus on community differentiation

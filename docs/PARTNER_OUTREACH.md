## Response Templates

### Partner Says "Yes"
```
Great! Let's schedule a 30-minute call to finalize details. 

I'll send over:
• Partnership agreement
• Brand assets needed
• Tracking setup instructions
• Launch timeline

Available this week?
```

### Partner Says "Maybe Later"
```
No problem! I'll follow up in [timeframe]. 

In the meantime, would you like to:
• Join our community as a member (complimentary)
• Receive our monthly newsletter
• Get early access to our partner case studies

Just let me know!
```

### Partner Says "No"
```
Thanks for considering us! If things change in the future, 
we'd love to reconnect.

I'll add you to our partner newsletter to keep you updated 
on our growth and new opportunities.

Best of luck!
```
